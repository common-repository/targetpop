A flexible, easy-to-use Pop-Up Maker! 

== Description ==

**Finally -- a flexible, *easy-to-use* Pop-Up Maker!**

>The uses for TargetPop are endless - use it to gain new subscribers, increase conversions, inform and entertain your visitors, advertise new products, showcase partner websites & products, and more!

**How does TargetPop work?**

You'll be up-and-running with attractive, effective Pop-Ups for your website in just two steps:

**1. Create a Trigger (the thing that causes a Pop-Up to appear to your visitors)**
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Name your Trigger
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Choose one of the 11 built-in Trigger types
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Fill in any applicable details for your Trigger (specific page/post, seconds until appearance, etc.)

**2. Create a Pop-Up**
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Choose one of the 7 built-in Pop-Up types
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Select a Pop-Up Template
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Create your Pop-Up contents and style it to your liking
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Choose a Trigger for your Pop-Up
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Set Pop-Up details - name, height, width, closing speed, etc.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Click the 'Create Pop-Up!' button

== Installation ==

**Method** **1)** Simply drop this folder (unzipped) into your WordPress plugin directory (/wp-content/plugins).

**Method** **2)** In the WordPress admin dashboard, click on ‘Plugins’ on the left-hand side, click on ‘Add New’ towards the very top of the page, click on ‘Upload Plugin’, and upload the ‘targetpop.zip’ file you downloaded.

**Method** **3)** In the WordPress admin dashboard, click on ‘Plugins’ on the left-hand side, click on ‘Add New’ towards the very top of the page, search for ‘targetpop’, and then click ‘Install Now’.
